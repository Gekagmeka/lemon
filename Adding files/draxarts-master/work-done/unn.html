Work done UNN

UNIVERSITY OF NIGERIA

How do you create a fitting website for a grand university that has become a hallmark of excellence and achievement?

We were briefed on the undertaking, clearly outlining the requirement to merge an academic resource with a virtual environment that would capture the sheer elements of the academia.

Eye to the detail.

The school encompassed 4 unique campuses, spanning 2 different states, 15 Faculties, over a hundred departments and a repository housing over 22,000 tomes and journals.

All of these distinctive aspects of the school were aptly represented, an ensemble of sorts that was fluid, efficient and thorough.

Highlights and Feature-rich.

A multi-featured enterprise, the website was filled with highlights like the Virtual Tour, a photo gallery featuring the endearing sights of the picturesque landscape.

The Virtual-Vector Map, a detailed, feature-richrepresentation of the main campus was developed and plugged into the site.

Multiple Layers of Information

Sub-sites were developed for the Faculties, all 15 of them.

More so, each Administrative Department, School of Studies, Unit, and Center were also crafted into the creative show of aca- demic and non-academic information hub for a full tertiary experience.

ALUMNI TRANSCRIPT PORTAL

We created the right Identity and interface for the Alumni Portal.

This was the terrain for the immense accumulation of past students of the great institution.

The Alumni was getting a much deserved portal for unfettered participation between the university and their alumni.

The interface was designed with care, precision and elegance.

It shadowed a fine line between the main school site, while somehow preserving its own identity. It opened doors to simpler methods for retrieving transcripts and sharing with several alumni around the globe.

Learning can always be fun and delightful.
