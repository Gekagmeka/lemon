Work done b&o

BANG & OLUFSEN

For a company with a solid pedigree, taking on the brief to further the web presence of Bang and Olufsen was a healthy challenge that we embraced willingly.

This was a strong luxury brand, and with our understanding of the online web marketing environment, we were poised for solid impressions.

The target was fixed on the urbane, mid-40s and above, with a healthy appreciation for enfolding their senses with otherworldly experiences, the kind Bang and Olufsen was well experienced with creating.

It was urgently important they see the firm a solid part of the local landscape, a homely feeling.

We integrated images used on the campaign with local elements and personalities.

This was the moment made from merging Danish products with ethnic undertones, reaffirming the focus on the indigenous clientele.

Marketing speakers that look like picnic baskets...

An audio device with volume controls that work like the spinning of a luxury car’s steering wheel...

TVs with a mind of their own;

this was a pleasant responsibility that corralled positive responses on the Social Media platforms.

“We basically had to break protocol, which usually was placing requests from the head office when people made orders. Now we just asked for more products to be shipped to take care of requests.”

B&O Lagos Office.

Our fansite was a direct touch, a base ground for the “faithfuls” to congregate, discuss, appreciate and interact on the product line that was the rave of the moment.

Working with a company full of delightful surprises naturally accounted for a passionate admiration for clients and admirers alike.
