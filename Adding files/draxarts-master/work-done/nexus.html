Work done NEXUS

NEXUS ALLIANCE

We were tasked to create a fresh website for Nexus.

This website project needed to complement their fresh perspective in the business terrain.

More so, we needed to highlight each arm of the company, emphasizing their role and embracing their brand principle in full effect.

A global perspective.

Nexus is a firm with partnerships all over the globe. And they required this perspective to reflect within their website.

Consequently, we infused the best web development technologies, with functional process that made the browsing experience a familiar one.

Green means Go

Putting their best foot forward, was to maintain the strong brand ideologies of Nexus.

We also emphasized their interest in the eco-system, weaving it into the imagery and thematic structure of the web project. We told their story in HTML, JavaScript and JQuery.

You really get a response.

This project was done with the understanding of the browsing habits of the local business environment.

We build the “Responsive” websites that intuitively know the device the user is browsing from.

This gives the user the full experience whether they’re using a PC, tablet or a smartphone.

The objective was simple.

A clean design which personified the sound business principles of Nexus.

A secure Content Management system was deployed to make the administration of their specialized content easy, yet protected from the inadequacies of weak deployments.

Direct perspective

The site shows Nexus focus on its clients and the general users, integrating Blogs, Social Media and functional contact options to make communication effective, and feedback an easy process.
